Privacy Policy

Status Saver by Vedge

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Overview

Status Saver ("the App") is developed by Vedge. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding your data.

Data We Collect

Data stored on your device only

  • Photos and videos: The App accesses WhatsApp status files through Android's Storage Access Framework (SAF) and saves copies to your device's local storage. These files are never uploaded to any server.
  • Edited media: Any edits you make (crop, filters, text, etc.) are saved locally on your device.
  • App preferences: Settings such as auto-save preferences and saved file records are stored locally using SharedPreferences.

Data collected by third-party services

The App uses the following Firebase services provided by Google:

  • Firebase Crashlytics: Automatically collects crash logs, stack traces, and device information (device model, OS version) to help us identify and fix bugs. This data is not linked to your identity.
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data such as app opens, feature usage, device identifiers, and diagnostics to help us understand how the App is used and improve it. This data is not linked to your identity.

These services may use your device's advertising ID for analytics purposes. For details on how Google handles this data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or messages
  • Financial or payment information
  • Account credentials

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. Crash and analytics data is processed by Google Firebase under their data processing terms. No other third parties receive your data.

Data Security

All data transmitted to Firebase services is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Media files are stored locally on your device and are protected by your device's security measures.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Saved and edited media: Delete any saved or edited file directly from the App's Saved screen (long-press to select, then tap delete). Files are permanently removed from your device.
  • All app data: Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data including preferences, cached thumbnails, and saved files in the App's directories.
  • Firebase data: Analytics and crash data collected by Firebase is anonymous and cannot be linked back to you. This data is automatically deleted per Google's standard retention policies (Crashlytics: 90 days, Analytics: 14 months).

If you have questions about data deletion or wish to request further assistance, please contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
